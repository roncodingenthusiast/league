About Us

At United Football Ottawa, we are driven by our love of the beautiful game. We pride ourselves on being dedicated to promoting quality, organized soccer leagues to the Ottawa area at both the recreational and competitive levels. Our number one goal is to provide you with a fun, sporting experience for everyone to enjoy while also cultivating a sense of pride and togetherness in the Ottawa soccer community for both men and women. At UFO, we are committed to giving you a safe environment to practice your skills and to increase your understanding of the game we all know and love.
Come join our league today, either as a team or as an individual, and become a part of our soccer organization and our community. Keep Calm and Play Soccer!
